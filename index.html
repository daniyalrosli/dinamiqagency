What is hibah

Hibah in Islam means the act of voluntarily granting ownership of property from one person to another when the settlor is still alive, without asking for anything in return/ reprisal (Iwad). It is different from a will as a will is granted to the beneficiaries after someone passes away.

Why is hibah important

Hibah is important because it allows a settlor or a property owner to distribute more of his wealth to anyone family members and non-family members, regardless of their religion. This is different from faraid where rights and the heirs portion of a deceaseds inheritance is based on the provisions of the Sharia Law.
